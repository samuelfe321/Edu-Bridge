About EDU-BRIDGE

Dedicated to accessible education for all.

Our Mission

EDU-BRIDGE is dedicated to removing barriers in education. We believe every learner deserves equal access to knowledge, regardless of physical, sensory, or cognitive ability.

Built with WCAG 2.1 AA compliance from day one, we support screen readers, keyboard navigation, text-to-speech, and adaptive layouts.
